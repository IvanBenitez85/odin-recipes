Description

This grilled pizza is topped with tomato sauce, tomatoes, black olives, roasted red peppers, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil. Of course, you can use whatever toppings you like!

How To Grill Pizza

Here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade pizza on the grill:
  1. Make the dough.
  2. Make the garlic oil, then brush it onto the grill grates.
  3. Shape the dough and place it on the hot grill.
  4. Flip the crust and brush with oil.
  5. Top the pizza however you like, close the lid, and cook until the cheese melts.

What To Serve With Grilled Pizza

Pair this grilled pizza with a side salad, such as Classic Restaurant Caesar Salad. If you want to put the hot grill to more use, try Grilled Garlic Bread or Grilled Romaine.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • ½ (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • ½ pinch white sugar
  • 1 ⅔ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ tablespoon chopped fresh basil
